Common questions

Before we build.

What is included in the monthly price?+

Your custom one-page website, domain and hosting management, mobile optimization, basic SEO setup, lead or booking integration, routine updates, security, and ongoing support.

How long does a website take?+

Most projects can move from a completed intake to a first draft in approximately one to two weeks. The final schedule depends on content, photos, approvals, and revision timing.

Can you connect my booking platform?+

Yes. We can connect common scheduling and booking services such as Square Appointments, Booksy, Squire, TheCut, Fresha, Vagaro, Calendly, and other platforms that provide a public booking link or embed.

Can I request changes after launch?+

Yes. Routine updates to text, images, services, prices, hours, and business information are included. Major redesigns or advanced custom features may require a separate quote.

Who manages the website and domain?+

OneTap Creative manages the website files, hosting, and domain during the active subscription. Transfer or buyout terms are handled under the signed client service agreement.

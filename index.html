Common questions

Before we build.

What does the $119 monthly price include?+

Your custom mobile-first one-page website, domain and hosting management, SSL security, basic SEO setup, lead or booking integration, routine content updates, and ongoing support. Advanced features or additional pages may require a separate quote.

How does the three-month commitment work?+

The initial commitment is three monthly payments of $119, totaling $357. After month three, the service continues month-to-month unless canceled before the next billing cycle according to your signed agreement.

How long does a website take?+

Most projects can move from a completed intake to a first draft in approximately one to two weeks. The final schedule depends on content, photos, approvals, integrations, and revision timing.

Who manages the website and domain?+

OneTap Creative manages the website files, hosting, and domain while the subscription is active. Transfer, buyout, cancellation, and early-termination terms are defined in the signed client service agreement.
